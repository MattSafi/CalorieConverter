How BMI is Calculated and What It Indicates

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on weight and height.
Interpreting the BMI Result:
Below 18.5 → Underweight
18.5 – 24.9 → Normal weight
25.0 – 29.9 → Overweight
30.0 and above → Obese
Note: BMI is a general guideline and does not account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition. For a more accurate health assessment, consult a healthcare professional.
